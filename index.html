Photo Clash

Fun photo competitions, with friends.
Get Started


add_circle

Create and Share

Create competitions and share them with your friends. Anyone with the link can take part by voting on the winner, even if they don't submit pictures.

publish

Submit Your Photo

You and your friends can submit one photo each. Photo Clash doesn't host your photos. Instead you just enter a public link to the image on Flickr, Facebook or where ever it's already online.

check_circle

Vote!

Remember, you can't vote for your own submission. After the competition ends you'll be able to see who's won. Photo Clash also gives you your own page, showing you all your entries, how many votes they received and which ones won.

Photo Clash

Photo Clash is a side project I've been thinking about and playing with for some time. While I've had several aborted attempts using different technology stacks, this version has a React frontend talking to a Rails API backend, all hosted on Heroku.
